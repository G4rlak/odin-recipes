Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a creamy and comforting Italian-American dish featuring tender slices of seasoned chicken breast served over fettuccine pasta, all enveloped in a rich and velvety Alfredo sauce made from heavy cream and Parmesan cheese.

The dish is often garnished with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan for added flavor and visual appeal. It's a quick and satisfying meal perfect for any night of the week.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare chicken: Season the chicken breasts with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  3. Cook chicken: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 6-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked and no longer pink inside. Remove chicken from the skillet and set aside to rest. Once cooled, slice the chicken into thin strips.
  4. Make Alfredo sauce: In the same skillet, add minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Season with salt, black pepper, and nutmeg (if using).
  5. Combine pasta and sauce: Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.
  6. Add chicken: Add the sliced chicken to the skillet and toss to combine.
  7. Serve: Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and additional Parmesan cheese if desired. Serve immediately.
Enjoy your delicious Chicken Alfredo Pasta!
