Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of stacked layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with fillings such as seasoned ground meat, cheese (typically ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and a rich tomato or béchamel sauce.

The layers are baked together until the top is golden and bubbly, resulting in a hearty, flavorful casserole that is both comforting and versatile. Lasagna can be customized with various ingredients, including vegetables, different types of meat, or even seafood, making it a beloved dish in many households around the world.

Ingredients:

For the Sauce:

  • Ground beef or italian sausage
  • Jar of marinara or pasta sauce
  • Minced garlic
  • Chopped onion

For the Cheese Mixture:

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Egg

For the Pasta:

  • Lasagna noodles (no-boil or regular, cooked as needed)

For Assembly:

  • Extra shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 190°C
  2. Cook meat: In a large skillet, cook ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add sauce: Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook until softened. Stir in the jar of marinara or pasta sauce. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Prepare cheese mixture: In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella, grated Parmesan, and an egg until well combined.
  5. Cook noodles: If using regular lasagna noodles, cook them according to the package instructions. If using no-boil noodles, skip this step.
  6. Assemble lasagna:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
    • Add another layer of meat sauce.
    • Repeat the layers (noodles, cheese mixture, meat sauce) until you reach the top of the dish. End with a layer of meat sauce.
    • Sprinkle extra shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan on top.
  7. Bake: Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let it rest: Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before serving to set.
Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
